About Me

I'm a doctoral candidate in the School of Computer Science at University College Dublin, funded by the SFI Centre for Research Training in Digitally-Enhanced Reality (d-real). My research focuses on Conversational Agents, Behavioural Economics, AI Ethics, Agentic AI, Generative AI and Computer Vision.

Currently working under the supervision of Dr. Vivek Nallur at University College Dublin and Prof. Vincent Wade at Trinity College Dublin, I explore how cognitive biases manifest in human-conversational agents interactions to further understand how human decision-making is shaped by AI agents.
